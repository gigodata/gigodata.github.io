## Definition

**GIGO Dataв„ў** is the public-facing company identity of **GIGO Data, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation founded by Adrian Wise Santos.

GIGO Dataв„ў is a data governance and AI integrity company. Its focus is the quality, provenance, and defensibility of information before it enters systems that make decisions вЂ” automated or otherwise.

The name derives from one of the oldest principles in computing: **Garbage In, Garbage Out**. AI did not create the problem. It removed the ceiling on its consequences.

> *What cannot be traced cannot be trusted. What cannot be trusted cannot scale.*

---

## The Problem GIGO Dataв„ў Solves

Most organisations do not fail because they lack output. They fail because the inputs beneath those outputs were never structured, verified, or governed strongly enough to justify the decisions later made from them.

In professional and organisational environments, records are typically:

- fragmented across tools and platforms
- captured for one purpose and reused for another
- undocumented in provenance вЂ” no record of who decided what, when, on what basis
- reconstructed after the fact rather than structured at intake
- treated as operational exhaust instead of evidentiary assets

When AI is layered on top of those conditions, the consequences do not merely persist вЂ” they scale. The error has no natural ceiling. An AI system amplifies whatever it is fed. Flawed inputs do not produce flawed outputs in isolation. They produce flawed outputs, faithfully, at scale, with confidence, across thousands of decisions simultaneously.

GIGO Dataв„ў exists to address that failure mode before it compounds вЂ” at the intake layer, before structure is lost and before provenance becomes irrecoverable.

---

## Operating Position

GIGO Dataв„ў works before the model runs:

- **Before** poor inputs become system behaviour
- **Before** undocumented decisions become institutional fact
- **Before** flawed records are amplified by automation
- **Before** outputs begin substituting for human judgment without defensible provenance

This is not a data cleaning business. Data cleaning is remediation вЂ” working on what has already gone wrong. GIGO Dataв„ў works at the governance layer: the conditions under which information is collected, classified, verified, and retained in the first place.

The question GIGO Dataв„ў is designed to answer is not "how do we fix bad data?" It is "how do we build systems where bad data cannot enter and accumulate undetected?"

---

## Core Focus

GIGO Dataв„ў addresses:

**Data quality and provenance** вЂ” what was collected, when, by whom, from what source, under what conditions, and whether those conditions were fit for purpose.

**Data governance** вЂ” the policies, structures, and accountability frameworks that determine how information is managed over time, not just at a point in time.

**AI integrity** вЂ” the chain of custody between the data that was used to build or operate a system and the outputs that system produces. AI systems are only as trustworthy as the data they were built on and continue to consume.

**Defensible documentation** вЂ” records that are not merely stored but structured, verifiable, and retrievable as evidence under operational, legal, and regulatory pressure.

**Compliance-aware design** вЂ” systems and methodologies aligned to applicable law across the United States (Delaware), the European Union (GDPR, EU AI Act), and Brasil (LGPD).

## Origin

GIGO Dataв„ў was not designed as a product and then applied to practice. It was named in a Paris apartment in 2011.

Adrian Wise Santos had just completed his graduate formation in menswear design at ESMOD Paris. His application for French citizenship was in progress. He needed a valid legal reason to remain in the country вЂ” so he began structuring a company and wrote a business plan.

A friend, Anne, was working on her own project beside him вЂ” on the floor of the same apartment, each on their own plan, present for each other without surveillance. One afternoon she asked him the simplest question: *what exactly do you do?*

He started to describe: systems, data quality, business rules, event logs, measurements, process. She listened politely and understood nothing.

At one point he thought aloud: *what I do is make sure there is no garbage at the input. GIGO. GIGO Data. Garbage In, Garbage Out.* He checked: was gigodata.com available? It was. He registered it on the spot.

Anne understood immediately.

If Anne understood, the name was right.

The French government later rejected his citizenship application and refused the company registration. Both. The idea survived both.

**Earliest documented GIGO Data business-plan / brand development:** January 9, 2013 (France)  
**First domain registration:** 2012 (registered while in France)  
**Delaware incorporation:** June 24, 2025  
**First public launch:** March 15, 2026

The delay between the Paris origin and the Delaware incorporation is not a gap in the record. It is the record вЂ” a provenance chain that predates the company by more than a decade, documented and retrievable.
